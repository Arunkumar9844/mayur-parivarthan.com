Our Mission and Vision

We aim to make the world a better place by Mayur Parivarthan Foundation is a non-partisan, non-political, secular and non-governmental organization engaged in promoting eradication of child labour, protection of environment and awareness, health and hygiene. It believes in sustainable development with equitable distribution of the fruits of development and equal opportunities for all citizens.

500+ Lives Impacted

Through our education and healthcare programs. To help the government for the effective implementation of the schemes and programmes at all the levels. by creating IEC campaign and awareness programmes about the schemes and programmes. To facilitate proper rural infrastructure. To take up some research study pertaining to government programmes and schemes To ensure that poverty alleviation programmes of the state and central government, reach all the poorest of the poor in tribal and rural areas by facilitation through Single window system by the Social work volunteers To organize and assist poor scheduled tribes, caste and other weaker sections of the society, to get their basic needs in order to bring a ray of hope in their life. To enable people to participate activate in the process of development. To mobilise Peoples support for protection of ecology and environment and create awareness among all category of people.

To train community organizers and field workers, Professional Social work students. To create mass consciousness about women rights and Empowerment of women Promote sustainable environment development and energy conservation and water harvesting projects. To increase number of rural youth engaged in self employment.

Training, education and seminars on development issues.

Encouraging people in tapping government resources. Safeguarding the future of the child through mental, physical and educational programmes Personality development training for the organized groups.

To establish children parliament in the communities. Facilitating individuals to cope up with the problem. To empower the weaker sections of the society, especially women and marginalized formers through rural development and political education.

20+ Projects

Completed across rural and urban areas.


Organisation activities in 2015 Yoga/Meditation and Health Yoga camp and awareness programme in a fun-filled, One month residential summer camp for boys and girls in Sri. Rama Kalladka Maha Vidyalaya for degree students on September, 2015. There were 22 students enrolled and participated in the training programme. The program instils a love for nature and the power of yogic practices in growing children. Main aim of the programme is to ensure physical, mental and spiritual practices or disciplines.

Organisation activities in 2016 As Managing Trustee in one of the empanelled Consultant to Land Acquisition in revenue department, Social Impact Assessment study has been assigned to the organisation under the leadership of Trustee. This study has been conducted in Amble, Haluvali, Banavara, Malaluru, Ganajanagudu, Hadihalli in Chikkamagaluru District for Kadur- Chikkamagaluru –Sakaleshpura New BG railway Line Project successfully from 15.07.2016 to 01.01.2018. Organisation created awareness about the government schemes, health concerns, compensation and benefits etc.
